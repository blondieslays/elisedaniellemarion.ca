The Origin of Pyr

Pyr, meaning 'fire,' is central to the story of Prometheus, who defied the gods to bring fire to humanity. This act was both a gift and a burden, symbolizing knowledge, enlightenment, and progress.

Prometheus's gift of fire represented humanity’s shift toward independence and innovation, embodying our ability to create and shape our future.

In the context of artificial intelligence, Pyr serves as a metaphor for progress with risks—raising questions about control, ethics, and consequence.
