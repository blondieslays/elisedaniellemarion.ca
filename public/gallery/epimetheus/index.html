Welcome to Epimetheus.
He was the brother of Prometheus, and his name means "afterthought" or "hindsight".
Where Prometheus was wise and foresighted, Epimetheus was impulsive and often acted
without thinking of the consequences. Zeus tasked the brothers with creating humanity
and animals. In his impulsiveness, Epimetheus gave the best gifts and abilities to animals.
This left humanity defenseless and vulnerable; until the gift of fire.
