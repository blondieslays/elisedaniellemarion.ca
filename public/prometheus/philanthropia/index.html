Philanthropia – Love of Humanity

Prometheus’s gift was not just fire but an act of philanthropy, born out of love for humanity. It demonstrated a selfless desire to uplift humankind.

This theme encourages reflection on how knowledge and technology, like AI, can be used for social good—fostering community, connection, and well-being.

AI has the potential to transform society, but it must be wielded with care to ensure it benefits all, much like Prometheus’s gift to humanity.
