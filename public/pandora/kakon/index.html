Kakon – The Unleashing of Evil

Kakon refers to the release of evil from Pandora’s box, marking the beginning of human suffering.

It highlights the importance of being mindful of the consequences that arise from technological progress—where the potential for harm is as real as the potential for good.

With AI, Kakon urges us to regulate and guide its development to avoid catastrophic outcomes.
