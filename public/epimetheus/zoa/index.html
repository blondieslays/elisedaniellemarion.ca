Zoa – Life and Creation

Epimetheus, unlike Prometheus, focused on animals, granting them unique traits for survival—strength, speed, and flight.

Zoa emphasizes balance in nature and the need to equip each being with the tools necessary for survival, a lesson relevant to how we build technologies today.

As AI continues to evolve, Zoa reminds us to focus on responsible creation—tools that help without harming the environment or society.
