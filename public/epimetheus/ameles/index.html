Ameles – The Consequences of Neglect

Ameles represents negligence, symbolizing Epimetheus's failure to think ahead when gifting animals their abilities.

It serves as a cautionary tale for today’s AI developers—reminding us of the risks when we prioritize speed and innovation over thoughtful design.

Neglect in the development of AI can lead to unforeseen challenges. Ameles warns us to stay vigilant and anticipate future consequences.
